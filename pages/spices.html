India is the world’s largest producer, consumer, and exporter of spices — supplying over 75 varieties to buyers in 180+ countries. The diversity of Indian climates and soils produces spices with unmatched depth of flavour, aroma, and pungency that drive demand from food manufacturers, retail brands, and wholesale distributors worldwide.

CM Trading & Exports sources spices directly from certified growing belts across South India — Red Chilli from Andhra Pradesh and Tamil Nadu, Black Pepper from Kerala’s highlands, and Coriander from Rajasthan and Madhya Pradesh. Every lot is cleaned, graded, tested, and packed to international export standards.

We offer both whole and ground forms across all varieties, with custom packaging in 25/50 kg bulk bags or private-label retail pouches. Our sourcing network ensures consistency in quality, pricing, and availability throughout the year.

Products We Export

Red Chilli

Dried whole & powder forms — Guntur, Byadagi varieties

  • Varieties available: Guntur (high pungency), Byadagi (high colour value), Wrinkled Teja (S17)
  • ASTA Colour Value: 60–160+ ASTA units depending on grade
  • Moisture: Max 12%; SHU (Scoville): 25,000–100,000
  • Available whole (de-stemmed, de-seeded options) or finely ground powder
  • Best for: food manufacturers, restaurant supply, retail brands
Guntur Chilli Byadagi Chilli Teja S17 De-Seeded Powder

Black Pepper

Whole peppercorns & ground pepper — Kerala origin

  • Kerala Malabar Black Pepper — globally recognised for bold aroma and sharp pungency
  • Bold size: 4.5mm, 5mm, 5.5mm screen; 500g/l bulk density typical
  • Volatile oil: Min 2.0% ml/100g; Piperine: 5.5%–9.0%
  • Available as whole peppercorns (garbling A or B) or machine-ground to mesh spec
  • Best for: fine dining supply, food processing, spice blends, retail
Malabar Whole 5mm Bold Ground (40 mesh) Garbled Grade

Coriander

Seeds & powder — Rajasthan, Madhya Pradesh

  • Eagle and Badami varieties from Kota (Rajasthan) — largest coriander market in Asia
  • Volatile oil: 0.3%–1.0%; Moisture: Max 10%
  • Whole seeds: bold, round, light green-brown with citrusy notes. Cleaned & graded
  • Powder: 40–60 mesh evenly milled, full aroma retained
  • Best for: spice blends manufacturing, food processing, curry mixes
Eagle Quality Badami Seeds Whole & Split Powder 40 mesh
